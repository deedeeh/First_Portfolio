Who am I?

Dina is a self-taught front-end developer who is enjoying every single line of code she writes. Of course at some points I feel like smashing my laptop screen or even worse; the laptop, but I have never felt making the decision of career shift in web development is not the right choice for me.

I am always working on the balance between learning and writing clean and efficient code. Furthermore, responsive design is on the top of my list. I know the only way to become the full-fledged developer I want to be is by practice so I am on the right track!

When I am not coding I work on my physical and mental health by going to the gym or watch a nice movie at home and eat popcorn.
